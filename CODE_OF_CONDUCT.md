# Code of Conduct

## Community Commitment
The ActiveMQ Classic 6 community is built on collaboration, respect, and technical responsibility.  
All contributors are expected to help maintain a welcoming, professional, and quality‑oriented environment.

## Expected Behavior
- Communicate clearly, respectfully, and constructively.
- Provide accurate, verifiable, and well‑documented contributions.
- Receive feedback and reviews with openness and professionalism.
- Report issues with sufficient detail and without hostility.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing sensitive or unauthorized information.
- Introducing vulnerabilities, regressions, or malicious code.
- Disrupting maintainers’ work or ignoring project guidelines.

## Maintainer Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for:
- Guiding the community with fairness and transparency.
- Reviewing contributions consistently and thoughtfully.
- Intervening when inappropriate behavior occurs.
- Applying corrective measures when necessary.

## Enforcement
Violations of this Code may result in:
- Private warnings
- Temporary participation restrictions
- Removal from the project in severe cases

## Contact
For concerns or reports, open an issue with the `conduct` label or contact the maintainers privately.
